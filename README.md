# 📌 Overview

AlphaBoard AI is a **Multi-Agent AI Investment Research Platform** developed for the **InsideIIM × Altuni AI Labs – AI Product Development Engineer Internship Assignment**.

Traditional AI assistants rely on a single model to answer complex financial questions. AlphaBoard AI instead adopts a **committee-based approach**, where multiple specialized AI agents independently analyze different aspects of a company before reaching a final recommendation.

Each agent contributes domain-specific expertise—including financial analysis, market research, valuation, competition analysis, and risk assessment—before a final decision is generated by the Chairman AI. This architecture improves transparency, explainability, and structured reasoning compared to a single AI response.

The project focuses on demonstrating AI product engineering, workflow orchestration, modular system design, and an intuitive user experience.

# 🧠 How It Works

1. The user enters the name of a company.
2. The Research Planner initializes the analysis workflow.
3. Multiple AI agents independently evaluate different aspects of the company.
4. Bull and Bear analysts debate the investment thesis.
5. The Chairman AI reviews all findings and committee votes.
6. A final investment recommendation is generated with supporting reasoning and confidence.

---

# 🏛 Engineering Decisions

Several architectural decisions were made during development:

* Adopted a **multi-agent architecture** to improve explainability.
* Used **LangGraph** for orchestrating AI workflows.
* Selected **Gemini** for structured reasoning capabilities.
* Designed a modular architecture to simplify future expansion.
* Prioritized transparency by exposing AI reasoning and committee voting.
